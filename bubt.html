Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT) is a powerhouse of academic excellence that champions the freedom of learning. It has established a formidable reputation since its inception in 2003. The university has risen to one of the top ranking private universities in Bangladesh.

More about BUBT

The exception of BUBT lies in the fact that all the members of the Trustee Board are, educationists, professors and entrepreneurs of education. With the advent of their leadership the university is rapidly climbing to a place that observes a unique position of international standard.

More about BUBT Trust

At present 09 graduate and 14 undergraduate programs are being offered by eleven departments under five Faculties.
